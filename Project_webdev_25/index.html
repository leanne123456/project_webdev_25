More About Me

Future Goals: As a future Mathematics and Computer Science teacher, my goal is to create a vibrant, engaging, and innovative learning environment where students develop a deep appreciation for these subjects. I am passionate about making complex topics accessible through interactive lessons, real-world applications, and the integration of technology. My ambition is to equip students with the problem-solving skills they need for both academia and industry. I also plan to continue my own education, researching new teaching methods and advancing my expertise in educational technology.

Farming, which requires problem-solving and adaptability, has shaped my approach to tackling challenges in education.
